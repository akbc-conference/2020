General Logistics

How do I get access to the locked pages?

If you have haven't, please register for the conference. If you are an invited speaker or a workshop organizer, contact us for a discount code.

I have registered by have not received my credentials.

After registration, it can take us up to half a day to email you the credentials for this website. If it has been a full day since you registered, confirm you have not received an email from akbcconf@gmail.com. If you have not, email us.

Can I change my username and/or password?

Sorry, we do not allow you to change your username or password. If you really need to change your password for security reasons, email us.

How do I log in to Rocket.chat?

Please use the same username and password as for this website.

Papers

When will the paper videos be available?

The videos are available on the respective paper pages, so you can watch them anytime. They will also be streamed during the lightning talk sessions (see schedule).

How can I ask questions to the authors?

Questions/comments regarding individual papers can be posted to their corresponding chat channels, available on paper pages or in chat here. Authors can respond over chat or during poster sessions.

How can I interact with the authors over video?

Each paper is associated with a "poster" session, during which the authors will be available for live video chat in Zoom. Each paper also has a dedicated chat. Both of these are available from each paper's page.

Invited Talks

Are the talks pre-recorded?

No, the talks will not be precorded, but streamed live.

Will the videos be available later?

Yes, we will be recording the talks, and will release the videos after the conference.

How can I ask questions to the invited speakers?

Attendees will be able to ask/vote on questions for the speakers (using Sli.do, embedded in the live page). If you are using the Zoom webinar, you can use this link.

Hallway Chats

How can I chat with other conference attendees?

Each day of the main conference will have dedicated slots for hallway chats. Attendees can navigate the schedule to find Zoom links to join hallway chats for live interactive discussions over Zoom.

Workshops

Will the workshops have the same format as the main conference?

No, we have left the format for the workshops to the organizers. However, there will be a chat associated with each workshop. Find information about each workshop on their respective pages.
